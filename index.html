Connoisseurs of delicious french pastries

You're going to fall in love with our macarons

We're a home-based baking business that specializes in the making of French macarons, a gluten- free pastry item made from ground almonds. Our shop has been recognized as the connoisseurs of this delicious French pastry because of the wonderful variety of flavors from our great master chefs.

Macarons made by hand with fresh ingredients

Renowned macarons, French delights can be met in a variety of flavors and colors and are brilliant precisely because of their simplicity - a crispy coating, but delicate in a loose blanket jam, chocolate butter cream spread is inviting, and combines perfectly with champagne or white wine.
